Match

Here you can select two strategies for each agent and run a match of the Iterated Prisoner's dilemma. A match consists of two players interacting with each other over a number of rounds. In each round a player may choose to cooperate or defect, the choice of what the player does depends on their selected strategy and the previous interactions in the match. Select a strategy for each of the players, type in the number of rounds you wish them to interact over and press run to initiate a match. Once finished you will be redirected to a page with the outcome and statistics produced by the interactions. If you're unsure of any strategies have a look at the descriptions in the information panel at the bottom.
