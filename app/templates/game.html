Game

A game consists of two agents interacting with each other over a number of rounds. Each round an agent may choose to cooperate or defect, the choice of what the agent does depends on their selected strategy. In a nature engine game you may select a strategy for each agent and run the game. Select a strategy for each of the agents and press run to initiate a game, once finished you will be redirected to a page with the outcome and statistics produced by the game. If you're unsure of any strategies have a look at the descriptions in the information panel at the bottom.
