Communities

Communities is a new concept. Communities contain agents, and have tournaments between their member agents. Each community nominates a "leader strategy" to go onto a higher stage and partake in tournaments with the other communities' leader strategies. As in our world communities go through generations, some strategies are more successful so reproduce more within the community and others are less successful so reproduce less. A leader strategy may change between generations for a community, and the success of the community is measured by the success of their leader strategy. The size of a community fluctuates depending upon this success.
